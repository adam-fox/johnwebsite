Ceremony & Reception

Our wedding ceremony will take place at 4:30 p.m. on Sunday, July 3, 2016 at the Illinois Beach Resort in Zion, IL. Weather-permitting, our ceremony will take place outdoors. An adult reception in the resort’s Grand Vista Ballroom will follow the ceremony.

The Illinois Beach Resort is located inside the 4,000-acre Illinois Beach State Park. The area is one of the most diverse terrains in the state, housing sand dunes, prairies, oak forests, the Dead River, and more than six miles of Lake Michigan shoreline. More information about the park and recreation opportunities can be found through the Illinois Department of Natural Resources.

Our Story

We met in the summer of 2012 at a party thrown by our mutual friends, Matt and Amanda Woodhead. The couple was throwing an end-of-season party for Matt and John’s softball team, and after Amanda mentioned “There’s a bunch of men at my house,” it really didn’t take much convincing to get Ally over to keep her company.

Even though there were several strapping young gentlemen at the party, something between us just clicked. Perhaps it was John’s determination to bring Ally and Amanda into the group conversation (about baseball trivia), or perhaps it was Ally’s ability to actually participate (The Oakland A’s were once based in Kansas City, FYI). But regardless of the how’s and why’s, a relationship blossomed. And like true Millennials, we added one another on Facebook and proceeded to message on the social network for a month before finally going on a real-life date. We were pretty inseparable from there on out, and the rest, as they say, is history.
